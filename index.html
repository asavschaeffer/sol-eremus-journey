Interactive Media

The "gamer" is not what it used to be.

An entertainment activity that was once tied to a physical board with intentional pieces has been digitized with computers, and transfered to console, mobile, and into AR.

Your grandfather plays gin-rummy, chess, and words-with-friends. Your little sister plays stardew valley and Minecraft on her iPad. Your boss plays candy crush on the subway to work. Hardcore gamers are now the minority of people who play games.

The potential of engaging with media to increase receptivity is limitless. The ability for a game IP to create a massive culture around the story, visual & auditory media is greater than cable, netflix, the library.

Companies across the board are realizing this. TikTok announced their objective of pivoting into a gaming platform. U.S. education is gamifying classwork.
